### Miku Expo 20XX
Miku Expo is the global concert tour involving Crypton Vocaloids especially Hatsune Miku.

Best things:
- Global Fandom Energy.
- 3D projection stage setup or an LED screen.
- Setlist variety from different composers and artists.
- Cosplays and handmade merch.
- More like a celebration of internet music that a standard concert.

### Georgia's Bakeries
Georgia has one of the most underrated food cultures in the world.

Top 3 Breads:
- Shotis Puri.
- Adjarian Khachapuri.
- Kubdari.

Best features:
- Tone oven Baking.
- Fresh from the bakery ofcourse.

### Japan's Konbini
Japanese convenience stores that are better than most countries.

Major chains:
- 7-Eleven Japan
- Lawson
- FamilyMart

Best features:
- High-quality ready to eat meals.
- Hot Snacks like Famichiki
- Seasonal desserts and limited edition foods.
- Useful Services especially Wi-Fi.
- Like any other, 24/7 reliability.
- Clean.

### Counter-Strike Major Tournaments
The biggest events in Counter Strike officially sponsored by Valve.

Best Experiences:
- Arena shockwave especially when the crowd erupts.
- Play body languages like slamming a desks.
- Sound Design, Pyrotechnics, Lightings.
- The silence of the crowd when a one versus one happens.
- Seeing the Trophy in Front.
- Random Encounters between fans or players.

### Tokyo's Comiket
Comiket or Comic Market is the world's largest fan convention held in Tokyo.

Best things:
- Doujinshi or Independent fan works that you can't find anywhere.
- Thousands of professional high-quality cosplayers.
- Buying and meeting with artists themselves.
- Massive attendance with like-minded fans.
- Community-driven filled with fan passion.
