Future Goals? I'm Just Trying to Get a Tomorrow.

The future is scary. But one thing that scares me the most is... A JOB APPLICATION. Touching that thing feels like your future is set for slavery. Jokes aside, the future is overwhelming. Not having set goals makes the path seems so blurry. Its like treading a forest with no destination. You may deviate from the path but I'll assure you that it is part of the process.

John Gabrielle Valencerina
Thursday, January 29, 2026
3 min read
What are your future goals?

Makes me think twice before answering. An answer that defines if either I'm lying by setting a path to something as long as I have an answer, or by playing it cool by saying I don't know and move on, or just setting a huge goal and dreaming big with higher risks and effort.

After my eighth grade in highschool. Whenever someone asks me what is my future goals. I would usually answer software development like it is already the path I wanted. I got to know the career itself when I was younger. Having to code solutions from problems in either games or websites. It makes me feel alive.

Two years now in my current career path treading the university stride in the course: Computer Science. A course that can either put you behind Mcdonalds counter or give you a salary slave job where both is 24/7. It is a gamble yes. If I deviate from my studies a little and the path will close on me. It is like hammering an iron over and over without losing concentration that would either fail and leave a big dent, or succeed.

Future goals? Small stuff. Learn, get experience, graduate. The target is to get a tomorrow. As long as the grind is worth it then it's fine. The future may be scary but also unfinished. Ask me again in a few years and I might say the same thing or maybe something completely different.
