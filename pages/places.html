Georgia's Bakeries

Bread, the 2nd best staple food as an asian, would taste best in bakeries honed with it. One of those bakeries is in Georgia and ofcourse would be a dream to visit. With enough time, money and love for the bread, I would be able to?

Tokyo's Comiket

Comiket, also known as Comic Market, is a semiannual convention in Tokyo filled with self-published works. As a person who loves reading instead of watching, this place is a to-go for me.

Japan's Konbini

Heard that convenience stores in Japan is too convenient. I want to taste the experience myself so why shouldn't I? Although we already have it anywhere else, why not try the best?

Counter Strike Major Tournaments

As a man who had been playing Counter Strike since its golden days, watching every tournaments like a sports channel. It is a dream to go to a tournament as an audience watching the stage itself not limited to the screen.
