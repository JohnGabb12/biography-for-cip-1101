Tokyo Comiket: Why I Like It

Comiket, also known as Comic Market, is a semiannual convention in Tokyo filled with self-published works. As a person who loves reading instead of watching, this place is a to-go for me.

It is the world's largest fan convention held in Tokyo.

    Best Things
  • Doujinshi or Independent fan works that you can't find anywhere.
  • Thousands of professional high-quality cosplayers.
  • Buying and meeting with artists themselves.
  • Massive attendance with like-minded fans.
  • Community-driven filled with fan passion.
